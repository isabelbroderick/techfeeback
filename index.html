Welcome to Pest Pursuit!
It's really important to stop pests because they eat the eggs and babies of our native birds. This makes it hard for the birds to have babies and grow in numbers. By getting rid of pests, we help our native birds stay safe and have more babies. This keeps our environment healthy and balanced.

starting on level 1 is recomended
